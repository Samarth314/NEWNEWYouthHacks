What is Youth Expo?



Youth Expo is the organization that brings children from grades 4-7 to participate in a tech day which includes 4 main workshops along with other activities such as guest speakers and fun games relating to tech. We were motivated to start Youth Hacks after we realized the importance of educating the future of technology to the youth as it provides a strong foundation for those interested in joining technology-related jobs.

What we offer at events

Robotics Workshop



We will use the Lego Mindstorms EV3 platform to educated kids on basic parts, programming tecniques, and we will even give kids the chance to test their robot on this year's mission (City Shaper).

CAD and Design Workshop



In this workshop, students will be given the chance to learn the basics of modeling structures on a CAD software and will also learn how to 3D print their very own design.

Coding (Python) Workshop



Students will be given the chance to learn the basic syntax of Python and will also get the chance to create a project that can be used in the real world. The winner gets a prize!

Virtual Reality Workshop



In this workshop, students will be designing their very own VR compatible app and will also be given a headset to run it on. The best and most creative projects get prizes!
